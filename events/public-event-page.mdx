Each event can have a **public page** that you can share so people can view event details, and in some cases RSVP or pay, without being logged in (depending on configuration).

## Get the public event link

1. Open the **event** in the Events list.
2. Find **View public page** or **Share** (or copy the **event URL**). The URL is typically something like `rowhouse.co/events/[eventId]` (or with your org slug).
3. Share this link by email, social, or your website.

<Tip>
Use the public page for open events (e.g. fundraisers, mixers) where you want anyone with the link to see details. For invite-only events, rely on the [invite emails](/events/invite-members) so only invitees get the personal RSVP/pay link.
</Tip>

## What the public page shows

- **Event title**, **date/time**, **location**, **description**
- Optionally a **Register** or **RSVP** button (which may create an invite or lead to the pay flow for paid events)
- Optionally an **Add to calendar** (iCal) link. See [iCal](/events/ical).

Behavior (e.g. whether strangers can RSVP or only view) depends on your app and event settings.

## Event discovery

- Some setups list **upcoming events** on your org's public site or a club page. That list may link to these public event pages. See [Website](/website/overview) and your pages for how events are displayed.
