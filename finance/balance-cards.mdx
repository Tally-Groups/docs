**Platform cards** (or **balance cards**) are a planned feature that let you issue cards or balance accounts for members (e.g. for use at events or for reimbursements). This feature may be **coming soon** and not yet available for all plans.

## What to expect (when available)

- **Balance cards**: Cards or balance accounts tied to a member, funded by your org (or by the member). Use cases may include event spending, reimbursements, or controlled spending.
- **Where**: If enabled, you will see **Platform Cards** (or **Balance cards**) under **Finance** in the sidebar. Setup and usage will be documented when the feature is released.

## If you do not see it

- The feature may be in beta or not yet launched. Check [Plan limits](/guides/plan-limits) and release notes, or contact support to ask about availability and roadmap.
