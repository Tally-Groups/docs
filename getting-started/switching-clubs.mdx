If you belong to more than one organization (club, chapter, or team), use the **club switcher** to change which one you're viewing and managing.

## Where to find the club switcher

- At the **top of the sidebar**, below the Rowhouse logo, you'll see the current organization's name and logo (or initials).
- Click that area to open the switcher dropdown.

## What the switcher shows

- **Current org**: Name and your role (e.g. Admin, Member).
- **Other orgs**: A list of all organizations you're a member of. Click one to switch.
- **Create Club**: Opens the flow to [create a new organization](/getting-started/create-organization).
- **Join Club**: Opens the flow to [join an organization](/getting-started/join-organization) with a join code.

## After switching

- The sidebar, dashboard, and all data (members, events, dues, etc.) update to the selected organization.
- Your **role** (admin vs member) is per organization. You might be an admin in one club and a member in another.

<Tip>
Bookmark or pin the dashboard URL for each org if you switch often. The URL may include the org context.
</Tip>

If you don't see an organization you expect, see [Troubleshooting](/getting-started/troubleshooting).
