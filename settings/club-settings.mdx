**Club settings** (organization settings) control how your organization appears and how people can join. Only **admins** can edit these.

## Where to find it

- **Settings** in the sidebar (admin only). You may see **General**, **Organization**, or **Club** as the main section.

## What you can change

- **Name**: The full name of your organization (e.g. "Economics Club"). Shown in the club switcher, emails, and public pages.
- **Logo**: Upload a **logo** image (used in the sidebar, storefront, emails). Use a square or near-square image; the app may resize. See [Troubleshooting](/settings/troubleshooting) if upload fails.
- **Email**: Contact email for the org (e.g. for "Contact us" or sender display). Optional.
- **Description**: Short description of the org (for public pages or directory). Optional.
- **Website**: External website URL (e.g. your existing site). Optional.
- **Join code**: A code members or invitees use to [join the organization](/getting-started/join-organization). See [Join code and invite](/settings/join-code-and-invite).

<Note>
The org **slug** (URL identifier) is usually set at creation and may not be editable, or may be editable only with care (URLs can break). Check the UI before changing the slug.
</Note>

## Save

- After editing, click **Save** (or **Update**). Changes apply immediately. Logo and name updates may take a moment to appear everywhere (e.g. cache).
