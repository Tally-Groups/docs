**Calendars** let you group events (e.g. "Social events", "Meetings") and optionally define a set of **calendar members** who are default invitees for events on that calendar.

## What is a calendar?

A **calendar** belongs to your organization and has:

- **Name** (e.g. "Social events")
- Optional **description**
- **Calendar members**: members who are automatically considered invitees for events assigned to this calendar (depending on app behavior)
- **Events**: events can be assigned to one calendar

## Create a calendar

1. Go to **Events** (or **Calendars** if listed separately) and open calendar settings or the calendar list.
2. Click **Create calendar** (or **Add calendar**).
3. Enter **name** and optional **description**, then save.
4. Add **calendar members** so they are the default invitees for events on this calendar (if the app supports it).

## Assign events to a calendar

- When [creating](/events/create-event) or editing an event, select a **Calendar** from the dropdown. The event is then grouped under that calendar and may automatically include the calendar's members as invitees (depending on implementation).

## iCal feed

- You can expose an [iCal feed](/events/ical) per calendar or per org so members can subscribe in Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, etc.

<Note>
Exact behavior (e.g. whether calendar members are auto-invited to new events on that calendar) may vary. Check the event invite step after assigning a calendar to confirm who is invited.
</Note>
