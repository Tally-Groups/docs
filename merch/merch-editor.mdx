The **merch editor** lets you add **block-based content** (text, images, buttons) to a merch group. This content is shown on the product or store page for that group (e.g. above or below the variant selector and add-to-cart).

## Where to find it

- Open the **merch group** (Finance → Merch → [group name]) and look for **Edit content**, **Page content**, or **Merch editor**. You may be taken to a Puck-style editor (same concept as the [website page editor](/website/page-editor) or [dues group](/dues/dues-groups) content).

## What you can add

- **Text**: Headlines, descriptions, sizing info, care instructions.
- **Images**: Product photos, lifestyle shots.
- **Buttons**: e.g. "Size guide" linking to a page.
- **Columns** or **sections**: Layout blocks to arrange content.

Content is saved with the merch group and displayed on the public [storefront](/merch/storefront) product page for that group.

<Tip>
Use the merch editor to add a short description, sizing chart link, or return policy so customers have the info they need before adding to cart.
</Tip>

## No code required

- The editor is drag-and-drop. You do not need to write HTML. Change or remove blocks anytime and save; the live store updates when you publish or save (depending on the app).
