Definitions of terms used in Rowhouse docs and product.

**Admin**  
A member with the **admin** role. Admins can manage the organization: members, groups, events, emails, website, dues, merch, settings, and subscription. Only admins see Settings and Subscription in the sidebar.

**Assignment (dues)**  
A single dues obligation: one member, one amount, optional due date and description. Each assignment has a status (unpaid/paid) and a unique **pay link**.

**Calendar**  
A named group of events (e.g. "Social events"). Events can be assigned to a calendar. Calendar members may be default invitees for events on that calendar.

**Club**  
Your **organization** in Rowhouse (e.g. a club, chapter, or team). The club has a name, slug, logo, members, events, dues, and settings. Used interchangeably with "organization" in the docs.

**Draft (email)**  
An email prepared for sending, built from a **template** with optional custom variables. You choose recipients and send from the draft.

**Dues group**  
A named set of dues (or merch) with optional **options** (tiers/variants) and block-based content. Type "dues" for dues; type "merch" for the store. Used when assigning dues or selling products so the pay/store page can show the right amount and content.

**Event invite**  
A record linking one **member** to one **event**: RSVP status, attendance, and (for paid events) payment status and amount. Invitees receive an email with a link to RSVP and pay.

**Group**  
A label or tag for members (e.g. "Board", "Class of 2026"). Members can belong to multiple groups. Groups are used to send emails or invite to events by group.

**Ledger**  
A record of financial activity: payments (dues, events, merch) and sometimes payouts. Used for balance and audit.

**Member / membership**  
One person's association with one **club**. A member has name, email, phone, role (admin or member), optional profile picture and custom column values, and can belong to **groups**.

**Merch group**  
A **dues group** with type "merch": defines a product with custom properties (e.g. size, color) and **options** (variants with prices). Powers the **storefront** and cart/checkout.

**Option (dues group)**  
A selectable tier or variant in a dues group: label and amount. For dues, options are tiers (e.g. "Standard $50"). For merch, options are product variants (e.g. "M / Blue" at $25).

**Organization**  
Same as **club**: the entity you create or join (name, slug, members, events, dues, settings).

**Pay link**  
The unique URL for a dues (or event) assignment that the member opens to pay. Sharing the pay link is how you collect payment from that member.

**Payout**  
A transfer of funds from Stripe to your connected bank account. Payouts follow Stripe's schedule (e.g. rolling or weekly).

**Slug**  
A URL-friendly identifier (lowercase, letters, numbers, hyphens). Used for the **club** (e.g. `my-club` in `rowhouse.co/my-club`) and for **pages**, **dues groups**, and **merch groups**.

**Storefront**  
The public store page where customers browse merch, select variants, and add items to the cart. Reached via the store URL (e.g. `rowhouse.co/store/org-slug`).

**Template (email)**  
A reusable email design (subject and body) with variables (e.g. `{{name}}`). You create **drafts** from templates and send to recipients.

**Transaction**  
A single payment record: who paid, amount, type (dues/event/merch), date. Viewed in Finance → Transactions.

**Variable (email)**  
A placeholder in a template or draft (e.g. `{{name}}`, `{{email}}`) that is replaced per recipient when sending. Custom variables are set per draft.
