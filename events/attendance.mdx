After an event, you can **mark attendance** so you know who showed up (attended) vs who did not (no-show). Attendance is recorded per event invitee.

## How attendance works

- Each event can have an **attendance link** (a unique URL with a token) that only admins or authorized users open.
- Opening the link shows the list of **invitees** (or RSVP "going"). You mark each person as **Attended**, **No-show**, or leave as **Pending**.
- Optionally, **attendance geolock** can require the device to be at a certain location to submit (if enabled for your plan).

## Get the attendance link

1. Open the **event** and go to **Attendance** (or **Mark attendance**).
2. Copy or open the **attendance link**. Share it only with people who should record attendance (e.g. yourself or other admins).
3. Open the link and mark each invitee as **Attended** or **No-show**, then save.

<Tip>
Use the attendance link on the day of the event (e.g. on your phone) so you can mark people as they arrive or at the end.
</Tip>

## Attendance statuses

- **Pending**: Not yet marked.
- **Attended**: Present at the event.
- **No-show**: Did not attend (e.g. RSVP'd but did not show).
- **Cancelled**: Invite or participation cancelled (if the app supports it).

## Geolock (if enabled)

- If **attendance geolock** is on, the person submitting attendance may need to be at the event location (or within a radius). This helps prevent marking attendance from elsewhere. If you are at the venue and geolock blocks you, check device location permissions and try again or contact support.

See [Troubleshooting](/events/troubleshooting) for attendance not saving or link not working.
