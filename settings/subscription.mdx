Your organization's **subscription** determines your **plan** (e.g. free, pro, premium) and what features you can use (emails, website, dues, merch, etc.). Only **admins** can view and change the subscription.

## Where to find it

- **Settings** → **Subscription** (or **Billing**), or use the **Subscription** link in the sidebar (admin only). You may be redirected to a billing portal or see the plan and upgrade options in the app.

## Plans

- **Free**: Limited features; many advanced features (emails, website, dues, merch) may be locked. See [Plan limits](/guides/plan-limits).
- **Pro**: Unlocks emails, website, dues, merch, and lower [processing fees](/finance/processing-fees). Billed monthly or yearly.
- **Premium**: Highest tier with more features and lowest fees. Billed monthly or yearly.

Exact plan names and inclusions depend on the product. Check the Subscription page and [Plan limits](/guides/plan-limits) for current details.

## Upgrade or change plan

- Click **Upgrade** (or **Change plan**) and select the plan and billing interval (monthly vs yearly). You may be taken to Stripe Checkout or a billing portal to enter payment method. After success, the new plan is active and locked features unlock.

## Billing and payment method

- **Payment method**: Stored with Stripe. Update it in the billing portal or the link Rowhouse provides (e.g. "Update payment method").
- **Invoices**: You may receive invoices by email from Stripe or Rowhouse. Download from the billing portal or Stripe Dashboard if needed.
- **Cancel**: If you cancel, you may keep access until the end of the billing period; then the plan reverts to free (or lapses). Locked features will be unavailable after that.
