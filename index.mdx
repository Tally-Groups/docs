Rowhouse helps you manage dues, members, events, and payments in one place. Stop juggling spreadsheets and multiple apps.

## Who it's for

- **Clubs and chapters** (student organizations, fraternities, sororities)
- **Teams and committees** that collect dues and run events
- **Any organization** that needs member management, event RSVPs, and payment collection
