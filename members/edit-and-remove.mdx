Edit a member to change their name, email, phone, role, or custom column values. Remove a member when they leave the organization.

## Edit a member

1. Go to **Members** and click the member (or **Edit** on their row).
2. Update **name**, **email**, **phone**, **role** (admin/member), and any [custom columns](/members/custom-columns).
3. Click **Save**. Changes apply immediately.

<Tip>
If you change their email, any future emails or pay links sent from Rowhouse will use the new address. Past sent emails still show the old address in history.
</Tip>

## Promote to admin

1. Open the member for editing (as above).
2. Set **Role** to **Admin**.
3. Save. They will now see **Settings** and **Subscription** and can manage the org. See [Roles and permissions](/members/roles-and-permissions).

## Remove a member

1. Go to **Members** and open the member.
2. Find **Remove** or **Remove from organization** (often at the bottom or in a menu).
3. Confirm. The member is removed from the organization's member list.

<Warning>
Removing a member does not refund dues or event payments they already made. It only removes their membership record. Their payment and RSVP history may still appear in transactions and event lists for accounting purposes.
</Warning>

- **Dues**: Unpaid dues assigned to them may remain (e.g. marked unpaid). You can [waive or cancel](/dues/waiving-and-cancelling) those assignments if needed.
- **Events**: Their event invites and RSVPs may remain in event data for history. You can leave as-is or adjust in the event's invite list if the UI allows.
