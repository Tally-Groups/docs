User preferences apply to **your account** across all organizations you belong to (unlike [Club settings](/settings/overview), which are per organization).

## Open user preferences

1. Click your **avatar** or **name** at the bottom of the sidebar.
2. In the menu, click **User Preferences** (or **Preferences**). You'll be taken to the preferences page.

## What you can change

- **Name and email**: Update the name and email associated with your account. Email may require verification.
- **Profile picture**: Upload or change your avatar. This appears in the sidebar and may be shown to other admins in the same org.
- **Theme**: Switch between **light** and **dark** mode. The choice is saved for your browser/device.

<Note>
Theme is stored in your browser (e.g. localStorage). If you use another device or clear data, you may need to set it again.
</Note>

## Dark mode

- In the same user menu (avatar at bottom of sidebar), you can toggle **Dark mode** / **Light mode** without opening the full preferences page.
- This affects only the Rowhouse dashboard UI, not your org's public pages (those use your website/theme settings).

For organization-level settings (name, logo, join code, subscription), see [Settings](/settings/overview).
