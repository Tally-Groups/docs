Create a Rowhouse account to manage or join organizations (clubs, chapters, teams).

## Sign up with email

1. Go to [Rowhouse](https://rowhouse.co) and click **Sign up** (or **Get started**).
2. Enter your **email address** and **password**.
3. Click **Sign up**.
4. Check your inbox and click the **verification link** we send. You must verify your email before you can use all features.

<Warning>
If you don't see the verification email, check spam and promotions. Add our sender to your contacts to avoid future delivery issues.
</Warning>

## Sign up with Google

1. On the sign-up or login page, click **Continue with Google**.
2. Choose your Google account and approve access.
3. You're signed in; no separate email verification is required.

<Tip>
Using Google is faster and avoids managing another password. You can still be invited to or create multiple organizations.
</Tip>

## After sign-up

- **Create an organization**: Use [Create an organization](/getting-started/create-organization) to set up your club or chapter.
- **Join an organization**: If you have an invite link or join code, see [Join an organization](/getting-started/join-organization).

If you already have an account, see [Log in](/getting-started/log-in).
