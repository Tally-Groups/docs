The **Emails** section lets you create reusable **templates**, build **drafts** (with custom variables), **send** to members or groups, and review **campaigns** (sent history) and open tracking.

## Main areas

- **Emails** (hub): Overview and quick send.
- **Templates**: Reusable designs (Puck editor or MJML) and subject lines with [variables](/emails/variables) (e.g. `{{name}}`, `{{email}}`).
- **Drafts**: Create a draft from a template, edit content and [custom variables](/emails/variables), then [send](/emails/sending) to [recipients](/emails/recipients) (list, group, or all members).
- **Campaigns**: View [sent history](/emails/campaigns) (who received what, status, and [open tracking](/emails/tracking)).

## Typical flow

1. Create a [template](/emails/templates) (design + subject + variables).
2. Create a [draft](/emails/drafts) from that template and set custom variables if needed.
3. Choose [recipients](/emails/recipients) (e.g. a group or all members).
4. [Send](/emails/sending). Emails are sent (often in the background for large lists).
5. Check [campaigns](/emails/campaigns) for delivery and [opens](/emails/tracking).

<Note>
Emails may be a **paid plan** feature. If you do not see Templates or Send, check your [subscription](/settings/subscription) and [plan limits](/guides/plan-limits).
</Note>
