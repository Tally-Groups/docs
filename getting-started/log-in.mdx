Sign in to access your organizations, members, events, and payments.

## Log in with email

1. Go to [Rowhouse](https://rowhouse.co) and click **Log in**.
2. Enter the **email** and **password** you used to sign up.
3. Click **Log in**.

## Log in with Google

1. On the login page, click **Continue with Google**.
2. Select your Google account. You must use the same Google account you used when signing up for Rowhouse.

## Forgot password

1. On the login page, click **Forgot password?** (or similar).
2. Enter your email and submit. We'll send a reset link.
3. Use the link to set a new password, then log in with your email and the new password.

<Note>
Password reset links expire after a short time. Request a new one if the link doesn't work.
</Note>

## Session and sign out

- You stay signed in until you sign out or your session expires.
- To sign out: open the **user menu** in the sidebar (your avatar at the bottom), then click **Sign out**.

If you can't log in or don't see your organization, see [Troubleshooting](/getting-started/troubleshooting).
