Rowhouse offers **free**, **pro**, and **premium** plans. Higher plans unlock more features, higher member limits, and lower processing fees.

## Member limits

- **Free**: Up to **25 members** per organization.
- **Pro**: Up to **200 members** per organization.
- **Premium**: **Unlimited** members.

If you exceed your plan's member limit, you must upgrade before adding more members. Downgrading is only allowed if your current member count fits within the new plan's limit.

## Features by plan

| Feature | Free | Pro | Premium |
|--------|------|-----|---------|
| **Members** | Up to 25 | Up to 200 | Unlimited |
| **Analytics** (website) | No | Yes | Yes |
| **QuickBooks export** | No | Yes | Yes |
| **Multi-chapter** | No | No | Yes |
| **AI insights** | No | No | Yes |
| **Dedicated support** | No | No | Yes |

<Note>
Emails, website (pages), dues, merch, and payouts typically require a **paid plan** (pro or premium). The exact locking of these features may be enforced in the app; when a feature is locked, you will see an upgrade prompt (e.g. in the sidebar or when opening the section).
</Note>

## Pricing (typical)

- **Free**: $0/month.
- **Pro**: e.g. $10/month (check the app or [Subscription](/settings/subscription) for current price).
- **Premium**: e.g. $25/month (check the app for current price).

Prices may be shown monthly or yearly (with a discount for annual billing). See the Subscription page in the app for exact amounts.

## Processing fees

- **Free**: e.g. 10% + 30c per transaction (dues, events, merch).
- **Pro**: e.g. 8% + 30c per transaction.
- **Premium**: e.g. 5% + 30c per transaction.

Exact percentages are defined in the app. See [Processing fees](/finance/processing-fees).

## Upgrade or change plan

- Go to **Settings** → **Subscription** and choose **Upgrade** (or **Change plan**). Select the plan and billing interval and complete payment. Your new limits and features apply immediately. See [Subscription](/settings/subscription).
