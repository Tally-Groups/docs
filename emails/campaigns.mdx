**Campaigns** (or **Sent** / **Sent emails**) is the history of emails you have sent. You can see which draft was used, who received it, delivery status, and (if enabled) who opened it.

## View campaigns

1. Go to **Emails** → **Campaigns** (or **Sent**).
2. You see a list of sends: draft name, send date, recipient count, and optionally status (e.g. sent, failed, pending).
3. Click a send to see **per-recipient** details: email address, status (sent/failed/pending), and if [open tracking](/emails/tracking) is on, whether they opened the email.

<Tip>
Use this to confirm delivery after a large send and to follow up with anyone who failed (e.g. invalid email) or did not open.
</Tip>

## Statuses

- **Sent**: Delivered to the provider (e.g. Resend); the recipient's server accepted it. It may still land in spam.
- **Failed**: Bounce, invalid address, or provider error. Fix the member's email in [Members](/members/edit-and-remove) and resend if needed.
- **Pending**: Queued or in progress (for background sends). Check again later.

## Open tracking

- If your plan and settings support it, **opens** are recorded when the recipient loads images or tracking pixels in the email. See [Tracking](/emails/tracking). Opens appear per recipient in the campaign detail view.

## Resend to failed recipients

- If the UI offers **Resend to failed**, you can resend the same draft only to recipients who had status **failed** after fixing their email addresses. Otherwise, create a new draft or send and select those members manually.
